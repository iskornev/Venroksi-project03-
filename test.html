As simple as A to Z

A: The flight Moscow-Los Angeles. Veeery long 12 hours. The most I can do to turn back time.
Z: That's an amazing how quickly you switch from important looking doctor to an artist alter ego. For one single flight!
A: Two sides of one medal, I guess. One keeps cool, another creates.
Z: But I still can't pronounce the russian name of this medal. The side which is serious.
A: Than name another one the way you are more comfortable.
Z: Probably I will simply flip the name vice versa.
A: There's beauty in simplicity.

*Z - is Zoobs, award winning artist who forges visual excellence in an embodiment of conceptual philosophy. Instagram: @theartistzoobs
*A - is me, when I'm in European continent. In the States I do as simple artworks as my taste possibly allows me.
